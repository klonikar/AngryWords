• This app asks for permissions like android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO, android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE.
  • These permissions are needed because media playing framework/APIs using by this app require these permissions.
  • The app is open source (on github) and it can be easily verified that the app does not record audio or read phone state.
  • The network access permission is needed to load the even offline web content like images and audio.
  • Although the app asks for Internet/Network, camera, device ID and call information permissions, it does not use them to record audio or connect to internet or determine device state. It is a completely offline app.
  • No private data is recorded, stored, or sent over the internet.
